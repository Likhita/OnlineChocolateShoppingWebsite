About Us

Bertha's Deluxe Chocolates offers a large variety of chocolate novelties, custom molded items and seasonal treats. There are chocolate heart boxes, heart-shaped caramels, Easter bunnies, chocolate Thanksgiving turkeys and chocolate santas for Christmas. Chocolate champagne bottles, birthday and thank you bars, and chocolate pops for every occasion make great gifts.

Favors for weddings, showers, anniversaries and parties are available. Businesses and organizations may have molds designed to create chocolate bars with their own logo. The possibilities are endless and the quality unparalleled
